About Us

You want to visit nature at the same time without losing comfort - please visit us. We do not compete either in a set of offersat a price: the balance of too modest value with good conditions is somethig that you can count on.

The estate "Grandfather of the Victor" is located in Yaremche, Ivano-Frankivsk region - it's Ukrainian Carpathians, with their unique landscapes, unique nature, conditions for active recreation and original national traditions. The mansion "Grand" Victor "has a convenient location - on the one hand is a quiet green place under the rock Elephant, which rises above the mountain river Prut with a cascade of rollovers - a natural analogue of the jacuzzi. From another, within 10-15 minutes walk you can reach the railway and bus stations , shops, markets, catering establishments of most municipal and state institutions, etc.
Fans of Carpathian nature will appreciate the proximity of the river and the forest, pure mountain air and crystal waterfalls.
